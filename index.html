ABOUT PATRICK MERRILL

Patrick Merrill is an Alabama Gulf Coast realtor who built his reputation the hard way — through honest dealings, sharp negotiation, and a relentless focus on getting clients to the closing table. He works across both residential and investment markets, guiding everyone from first-time buyers to seasoned investors through complex deals, hidden opportunities, and smooth closings.

Patrick launched LIFTED to share the conversations he wishes more agents were having. Drawing on his deep Gulf Coast market experience and a wide network of realtors and industry partners, he hosts a show that’s equal parts practical real estate strategy and honest reflection on the mindset, relationships, and decisions that separate short-term wins from lasting success.
